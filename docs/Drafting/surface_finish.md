# Surface Finish
## Surface Finish Applications

<!-- table-class: table-layout-auto table-divider-cols table-divider-rows -->
| Ra Surface Rating [µm] | Application |
|---:|---|
| 25 | Rough, low grade surfaces resulting from sand casting, saw cutting or rough forging. Rarely specified, but suitable for rough clearances in construction. |
| 12.5 | Rough, low grade surface for clearance areas in machinery, jigs, and fixtures. Natural surface for sand castings or rough forging. |
| 6.3 | Coarse production surface for unimportant clearances and clean up operations. Natural surface of forgings, extrusions, and permanent mouldings. |
| 3.2 | Roughest surface recommended for parts under load, vibration, or high stress. |
| 1.6 | Used for close fits, stressed parts, and bearing interfaces. |
| 0.8 | Suitable for stress concentration surfaces and bearing surfaces under light loads. |
| 0.4 | High quality surface for rapidly rotating shaft bearings, heavily loaded baring surfaces or extreme tension members. |
| 0.2 | A fine surface used in precision gauges, instrument work, or rapidly rotating surfaces where lubrication is not desirable. |
| 0.1 | A costly refined surface for instruments, gauges, and sliding surfaces where lubrication is not dependable. |
| 0.05 | A polished appearance for sensitive instruments, laboratory items and precision gauge blocks. |
