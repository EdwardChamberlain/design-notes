# Heat Transfer

## Purpose
Store thermal assumptions, boundary conditions, and back-of-envelope calculations.

## Key rules
- Separate conduction, convection, and radiation contributions.
- Keep units explicit for every thermal resistance term.
- Validate simple models against test or simulation checkpoints.

## Worked example
For 1D conduction through a wall, start with Fourier's law and bound expected temperature drop before detailed CFD.

## References
- Incropera et al., Fundamentals of Heat and Mass Transfer
- Material thermal conductivity datasheets
